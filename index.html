About Image Generation

Lumina AI uses a process called Diffusion. It starts with a field of random digital noise (static) and slowly refines it, guided by your prompt, until a clear, high-fidelity image emerges.

Our Flux engine calculates billions of parameters in milliseconds to ensure lighting, anatomy, and textures are photorealistic. Designed by Connectrajtiwari.
